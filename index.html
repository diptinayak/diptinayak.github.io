I am a Life Sciences Research Foundation Fellow courtesy of the Simons Foundation, and a Carl Woese Institute for Genomic Biology fellow at the University of Illinois. I received my PhD from the Organismic and Evolutionary Biology Department at Harvard University, under the direction of Prof. Chris Marx (2014), and my Masters degree from the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department at Stanford University (2009).
